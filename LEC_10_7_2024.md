# Bayes' Theorem
* Conditional probability: $P(A|B) = \frac{P(A\cap B)}{P(B)}$
* Bayes' Rule: $P(B|A) = \frac{P(A|B) * P(B)}{P(A)}$
* Let $B_1 \to B_n$ be a partition of $\Omega$
  * $P(B_i|A) = \frac{P(A|B_i)P(B_i)}{\Sigma _{l=1}^n P(A|B_l)P(B_l)}$
